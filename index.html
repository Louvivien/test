Welcome to Roybon Horse Riding Company

Located in the beautiful Rhône-Alpes region of France, Roybon Horse Riding Company offers a unique and unforgettable experience for riders of all levels. Our experienced guides will take you on a journey through the stunning countryside, with breathtaking views of the mountains and forests.

Our happy and playful chestnut mare enjoying a roll in the dirt.

Our Horses

Our horses are carefully selected for their temperament, training, and suitability for riders of all levels. We have a range of breeds, including French Trotters, Arabians, and Thoroughbreds, each with their own unique personality and characteristics. All of our horses are well-cared for and trained to ensure a safe and enjoyable ride for our guests.
