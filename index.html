Les 3 Vallées

Les 3 Vallées is the largest ski area in the world, located in the French Alps. It consists of eight interconnected ski resorts, including Meribel, Courchevel, and Val Thorens. With over 600 km of slopes, it offers endless possibilities for skiing, snowboarding, and other winter sports. The area is also known for its stunning mountain scenery and charming villages.

Roybon

Roybon is a charming village located in the Isère department of southeastern France. It is known for its beautiful architecture, including the 16th-century castle and the Romanesque church. The village is surrounded by lush forests and is a popular destination for hiking and cycling. It also offers a variety of local restaurants and shops.

Vercors Regional Nature Park

The Vercors Regional Nature Park is a protected area located in the French Prealps. It covers over 200,000 hectares of mountainous terrain and is home to a variety of wildlife, including chamois, ibex, and golden eagles. The park offers many opportunities for outdoor activities, such as hiking, rock climbing, and mountain biking. It also has several scenic drives and picturesque villages to explore.
