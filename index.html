Loja de Pao

Welcome to our bakery! We offer a wide variety of breads and pastries, made fresh daily using only the finest ingredients.

Our breads come in many different shapes and sizes, from classic baguettes and sourdough loaves to specialty breads like focaccia and challah. We also offer a variety of pastries, including croissants, danishes, and muffins, as well as cakes and pies for special occasions.

At Loja de Pao, we believe that good bread is the foundation of a good meal. That's why we take the time to make each loaf by hand, using traditional techniques and the best ingredients available. Whether you're looking for a simple loaf of bread for your morning toast or a show-stopping centerpiece for your next dinner party, we've got you covered.
