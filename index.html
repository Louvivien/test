Welcome to Roybon Horse Riding Company

Roybon Horse Riding Company is a family-owned business located in the heart of the French Alps. We offer horse riding lessons and guided tours for riders of all levels, from beginners to advanced. Our horses are well-trained and cared for, and our experienced instructors will ensure that you have a safe and enjoyable experience.

At Roybon Horse Riding Company, we pride ourselves on providing a friendly and welcoming atmosphere for our clients. Whether you are looking to improve your riding skills, explore the beautiful French countryside on horseback, or simply spend some time with our friendly horses, we have something for everyone.
