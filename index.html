Adopt a Puppy

Our rescue center has a variety of Pomeranian puppies available for adoption. All of our puppies are up-to-date on their vaccinations and have been spayed or neutered. Come visit us today and find your new best friend!

Adopt an Adult Dog

We also have adult Pomeranian dogs available for adoption. These dogs are already trained and make great companions. Come meet our adult dogs and see if one is the right fit for your family.

Foster a Dog

If you're not ready to adopt a dog permanently, consider fostering a Pomeranian. Fostering helps socialize dogs and prepares them for their forever homes. Contact us to learn more about our foster program.

About Pomeranians

Pomeranians are a small breed of dog that are known for their fluffy coats and friendly personalities. They are great family pets and do well in apartments or small homes. Pomeranians are also very intelligent and can be trained to do a variety of tricks.

If you're interested in adopting a Pomeranian, contact us today!
