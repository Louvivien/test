Welcome to Hair Force One

We are a hair salon located in St Marcellin, France. Our team of experienced stylists are dedicated to providing you with the best hair care services. Whether you need a simple haircut or a complete makeover, we've got you covered.

Our salon is equipped with the latest tools and equipment to ensure that you get the best possible service. We use only the highest quality hair care products to ensure that your hair looks and feels great.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of hair care services, including:

  • Haircuts and styling
  • Hair coloring and highlights
  • Perms and straightening
  • Hair treatments and conditioning
  • Special occasion styling

Our stylists are trained in the latest techniques and trends to ensure that you get the look you want. We also offer consultations to help you choose the best style for your hair type and face shape.
