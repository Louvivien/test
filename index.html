About Us

We are a horse riding company located in Roybon, France. Our company offers horse riding lessons, trail rides, and other equestrian activities for people of all ages and skill levels. Our horses are well-trained and well-cared for, and our staff is experienced and friendly. We are dedicated to providing a safe and enjoyable experience for all of our customers.

Whether you are a beginner or an experienced rider, we have something to offer you. Our lessons are tailored to your individual needs and goals, and our trail rides take you through some of the most beautiful countryside in France. Come and experience the joy of horse riding with us!

Our Horses

We have a variety of horses available for lessons and trail rides. Our horses are well-trained and well-cared for, and we match each rider with a horse that is suitable for their skill level and experience. Our horses are friendly and gentle, and they love to be around people.

Some of the breeds we have include the French Saddlebred, the Arabian, and the Thoroughbred. Each breed has its own unique characteristics and personality, and we will help you find the perfect horse for your needs.

Our Services

We offer a range of services to suit your needs. Whether you are looking for a one-time lesson or a long-term riding program, we have something for you. Our services include:

  • Horse riding lessons for all levels
  • Trail rides through the French countryside
  • Summer camps for kids
  • Birthday parties and special events

We also offer boarding services for horses, and we have a variety of stables available to suit your needs. Our staff is experienced in horse care and will provide your horse with the best possible care.
