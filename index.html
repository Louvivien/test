Welcome to Horse Riding Company in Roybon, France

We offer horse riding lessons and tours in the beautiful French countryside. Come ride with us and experience the thrill of the open road on horseback!

Our Horses

We have a variety of horses to suit riders of all levels. From gentle ponies for beginners to spirited stallions for experienced riders, we have the perfect horse for you.

Riding Lessons

Our experienced instructors offer riding lessons for all ages and skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced rider, we can help you improve your skills and become a better rider.

Horse Tours

We offer guided horse tours through the beautiful French countryside. Explore the scenic trails and enjoy the breathtaking views on horseback.

About Us

We are a family-owned horse riding company located in the picturesque town of Roybon, France. Our passion for horses and the outdoors is what inspired us to start this business, and we are dedicated to providing our customers with the best possible riding experience.

Our horses are well-trained and well-cared for, and our instructors are experienced and knowledgeable. We offer riding lessons for all ages and skill levels, as well as guided horse tours through the beautiful French countryside.
