Hair Force One

About Us

Hair Force One is a hair salon located in the heart of St Marcellin, France. Our team of experienced hairstylists is passionate about creating the perfect look for each and every one of our clients. We offer a wide range of services, including haircuts, coloring, styling, and more. We pride ourselves on providing a welcoming and relaxing atmosphere, where our clients can sit back, relax, and let us take care of the rest.

Our Services

Haircuts

Our hairstylists are experts at creating the perfect haircut for your face shape and hair type. Whether you're looking for a simple trim or a complete transformation, we've got you covered.

Coloring

From subtle highlights to bold, vibrant colors, our hairstylists can help you achieve the perfect look. We use only the highest-quality hair color products to ensure that your hair stays healthy and beautiful.

Styling

Whether you're getting ready for a special occasion or just want to look your best, our hairstylists can help you achieve the perfect style. From sleek and straight to bouncy curls, we've got you covered.
