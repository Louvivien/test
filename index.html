About Us

We are a horse riding company located in Roybon, France. Our goal is to provide the best horse riding experience for our customers. Our horses are well-trained and our instructors are experienced. We offer riding lessons for all levels, from beginners to advanced riders.

Our Horses

We have a variety of horses for different riding styles and levels. Our horses are well-cared for and trained to provide a safe and enjoyable riding experience. We offer horseback riding tours through the beautiful countryside of Roybon.

Riding Lessons

We offer riding lessons for all levels, from beginners to advanced riders. Our experienced instructors will teach you the basics of horse riding and help you improve your skills. We have both indoor and outdoor riding arenas, so you can ride all year round.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or would like to book a riding lesson or tour, please contact us by phone or email. We are open every day from 9am to 5pm.
