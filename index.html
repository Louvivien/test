Le site internet de l'office du tourisme de Roybon, France

Roybon is a small, picturesque village located in the heart of the French Alps, surrounded by beautiful mountains, forests, and lakes. It is the perfect destination for nature lovers, hikers, and adventurers, as well as for those who seek peace and tranquility away from the hustle and bustle of the city.

The main attraction of Roybon is the Château de Roybon, a magnificent medieval castle that dates back to the 13th century. The castle has been beautifully restored and is now open to visitors, who can admire its impressive architecture, explore its rooms and galleries, and learn about its fascinating history.

Other attractions in Roybon include the beautiful Saint-Michel church, the charming local market, and the numerous hiking trails that lead to breathtaking viewpoints and hidden gems.
