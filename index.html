Boulangerie L'ami du Pain à St Marcellin, France

Welcome to Boulangerie L'ami du Pain, a charming bakery located in the heart of St Marcellin, France. Our artisanal breads and pastries are made with the finest ingredients and baked fresh daily.

Indulge in our delicious croissants, baguettes, and pastries, all lovingly crafted by our talented bakers. Whether you're craving a classic pain au chocolat or a rustic sourdough loaf, we have something to satisfy every palate.
