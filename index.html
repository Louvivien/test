About Us

We are a horse riding company located in Roybon, France. Our mission is to provide high-quality horse riding experiences for riders of all levels, from beginners to advanced riders. Our team of experienced instructors and well-trained horses will ensure that you have a safe and enjoyable ride.

Whether you want to take a leisurely ride through the countryside, or improve your riding skills with our expert instructors, we have something for everyone. We offer a range of riding lessons and activities, including trail rides, jumping lessons, dressage lessons, and more.

Our horses are all well-trained and well-cared for, and we take pride in their health and happiness. We believe that happy horses make for happy riders, and we do everything we can to ensure that our horses are healthy and content.
