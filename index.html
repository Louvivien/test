Le site internet de l'office du tourisme de Roybon, France

Roybon is a charming village located in the heart of the French countryside. With its picturesque landscapes, friendly locals, and delicious cuisine, it's the perfect destination for anyone looking to experience the beauty and culture of France.

One of the highlights of visiting Roybon is the weekly market, where locals and tourists alike gather to shop for fresh produce, meats, cheeses, and baked goods. The market also features handmade crafts and souvenirs, making it the perfect place to find unique gifts and mementos of your trip.
