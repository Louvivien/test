About Us

At Roybon Horse Riding Company, we are passionate about horses and providing our clients with a unique and unforgettable riding experience. Our stables are located in the heart of the beautiful French countryside, surrounded by stunning scenery and miles of riding trails.

Our team of experienced trainers and guides are dedicated to ensuring the safety and enjoyment of our clients, whether they are beginners or experienced riders. We offer a variety of riding packages, from short trail rides to multi-day excursions.

We offer a range of horse riding services to suit all levels of experience and ability. Whether you are a complete beginner or an experienced rider, we have a package to suit you.

  • Lessons
  • Multi-day excursions
