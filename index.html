About Us

We are a horse riding company based in Roybon, France. Our mission is to provide our clients with an unforgettable experience of the French countryside on horseback. Our horses are well-trained and our guides are experienced and knowledgeable. We offer a variety of services, including horseback riding tours, lessons and training, and special events. We cater to all ages and skill levels, and we can customize our services to meet your needs.

Whether you are a seasoned rider or a beginner, we have something for you. Come and join us for a horse riding adventure that you will never forget.
