About Us

Roybon Horse Riding Company is a family-run business located in the heart of the French Alps. We offer horse riding lessons and guided tours for riders of all levels, from beginners to advanced. Our experienced instructors will help you improve your riding skills and discover the stunning landscapes of the region.

We have a wide range of horses to suit every rider's needs, from gentle ponies for children to spirited stallions for experienced riders. Our horses are well-trained and well-cared for, and we take great pride in ensuring their welfare and happiness.

At Roybon Horse Riding Company, we believe that horse riding is not just a sport, but a way of life. We are passionate about horses and the natural beauty of the Alps, and we want to share our love of both with you. Come and join us for an unforgettable riding experience!
