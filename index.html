Explore the Countryside

Join us for a horseback ride through the beautiful countryside of Roybon. Our experienced guides will take you on a journey through lush forests, rolling hills, and picturesque villages.

Meet Our Horses

We have a variety of horses to suit riders of all levels, from gentle ponies for children to spirited stallions for experienced riders. Our horses are well-cared for and trained with kindness and respect.

Experience Nature

There's no better way to experience the beauty of nature than on horseback. Our rides take you through some of the most stunning landscapes in France, where you can breathe in the fresh air and soak up the peaceful surroundings.
