About Us

We are a horse riding company located in the beautiful town of Roybon, France. Our mission is to provide our clients with the best possible experience when it comes to horse riding. We have a team of experienced instructors and a stable full of well-trained horses that are suitable for riders of all levels.

Our Horses

We have a variety of horses that are suitable for riders of all levels. Our horses are well-trained and well-cared for, and we take great pride in their health and happiness. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced rider, we have the perfect horse for you.

Riding Lessons

We offer riding lessons for riders of all levels. Our experienced instructors will work with you to improve your riding skills and help you achieve your goals. Whether you want to learn the basics of horse riding or you want to compete at a high level, we have the expertise and the facilities to help you succeed.
