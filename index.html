About Us

We are a horse riding company located in Roybon, France. Our mission is to provide safe and enjoyable horse riding experiences for people of all ages and skill levels. Our horses are well-trained and well-cared for, and our staff is experienced and knowledgeable. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced rider, we have something for you.

Our Horses

We have a variety of horses to suit every rider's needs and preferences. From gentle ponies for children to spirited stallions for experienced riders, we have it all. Our horses are well-trained and well-cared for, and we take pride in their health and happiness.

Riding Lessons

We offer riding lessons for people of all ages and skill levels. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced rider, we have a lesson plan that will suit your needs. Our lessons are taught by experienced instructors who will help you improve your riding skills and build your confidence.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or would like to book a riding lesson, please don't hesitate to contact us. You can reach us by phone, email, or by filling out the form on our website. We look forward to hearing from you!
