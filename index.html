Our Services

Horse Riding Lessons

We offer horse riding lessons for all levels, from beginner to advanced. Our experienced instructors will help you improve your riding skills and gain confidence.

Horse Trail Rides

Experience the beauty of the French countryside on horseback with our guided trail rides. We offer rides for all levels, from beginner to advanced.

Horse Boarding

We offer full-service horse boarding with top-notch care for your horse. Our facilities include spacious stalls, daily turnout, and high-quality feed.
