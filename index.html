Welcome to Horse Riding Company in Roybon, France

We are a horse riding company located in the beautiful town of Roybon, France. Our company has been providing quality horse riding experiences for over 20 years. Our horses are well-trained and our staff is experienced to ensure that every ride is a memorable one.

Whether you are a beginner or an experienced rider, we have a ride for you. We offer a variety of rides, from short rides through the countryside to full-day rides in the mountains. Our rides are suitable for all ages and abilities.

Our company is dedicated to providing the best possible horse riding experience for our clients. We take great care in selecting our horses and training our staff to ensure that our clients have a safe and enjoyable ride. We also offer riding lessons for those who are interested in learning how to ride or improving their riding skills.
