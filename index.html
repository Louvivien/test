Welcome to our Horse Riding Company in Roybon, France

We are a premier horse riding company based in Roybon, France. Our company offers a wide range of horse riding services to suit all levels of riders, from beginners to experienced riders.

Our experienced and friendly instructors will guide you through the beautiful French countryside, where you will experience the natural beauty of the region while riding our well-trained horses.

Our Services

We offer a variety of horse riding services to suit all levels of riders:

  • Guided trail rides through the beautiful French countryside
  • Lessons for beginners and experienced riders
  • Horse boarding and training
  • Special events, including birthday parties and corporate team-building activities
