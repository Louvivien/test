Welcome to our Horse Riding Company in Roybon, France

About Us

Our horse riding company is located in the charming town of Roybon, France. We have a team of experienced guides and a stable of well-trained horses to ensure that your ride is safe, enjoyable, and unforgettable. Whether you're looking for a leisurely ride through the countryside or an adrenaline-fueled gallop, we have the perfect horse and route for you.

Our horses are well-cared for and trained using natural horsemanship techniques. We believe in treating our horses with respect and kindness, and we expect the same from our riders. We offer a range of rides, from short treks to multi-day adventures, and we can customize your ride to suit your needs and preferences.

Our Horses

We have a variety of horses to suit riders of all levels and preferences. Our horses are well-trained, well-cared for, and have unique personalities and quirks that make them a joy to ride. Whether you're looking for a gentle, easy-going horse or a spirited, energetic one, we have the perfect horse for you.

Our horses are trained using natural horsemanship techniques, which means that they are responsive, willing, and respectful. We believe in treating our horses with kindness and respect, and we expect the same from our riders. We have horses of all breeds, sizes, and colors, and we can match you with the perfect horse for your ride.
