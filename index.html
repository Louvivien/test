About Us

We are a horse riding company located in Roybon, France. Our mission is to provide our clients with the best horse riding experience possible. We have a team of experienced and passionate riders who will guide you through the beautiful countryside of Roybon.

Our Horses

We have a wide variety of horses to suit all levels of riders. From gentle ponies for children to spirited stallions for experienced riders, we have the perfect horse for you. All of our horses are well-trained and well-cared for, and we take great pride in their health and happiness.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or would like to book a ride with us, please don't hesitate to get in touch. You can reach us by phone, email, or through our website. We look forward to hearing from you!
