About Us

We are a family-owned horse riding company located in Roybon, France. Our mission is to provide high-quality horse riding lessons and experiences to riders of all levels. We believe that horses are amazing animals and we want to share our passion for them with you.

Our Horses

We have a variety of horses to suit all riders. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced rider, we have the perfect horse for you. Our horses are well-trained and well-cared for, and they love to be ridden.

Riding Lessons

We offer riding lessons for all levels, from beginners to advanced riders. Our instructors are experienced and passionate about teaching, and they will help you improve your riding skills and achieve your goals.
