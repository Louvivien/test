About Us

We are a horse riding company located in the beautiful town of Roybon, France. Our experienced instructors and well-trained horses provide a safe and enjoyable horse riding experience for riders of all levels. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced rider, we have a program that fits your needs.

Our facilities include a large indoor arena, outdoor riding areas, and a comfortable lounge for riders and their families.

Our Services

We offer a variety of horse riding programs, including:

  • Lessons for beginners and advanced riders
  • Trail rides through the beautiful French countryside
  • Training for horses and riders
  • Boarding for horses
