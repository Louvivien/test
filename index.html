About Us

We are a family-owned horse riding company located in the beautiful countryside of Roybon, France. Our mission is to provide our clients with an unforgettable horse riding experience that they will cherish for a lifetime.

Our horses are well-trained and well-cared for, and our experienced instructors will guide you through the beautiful trails of Roybon, France. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced rider, we have the perfect horse riding package for you.

Our Services

Horse Riding Lessons

We offer horse riding lessons for beginners and advanced riders. Our experienced instructors will guide you through the basics of horse riding and help you improve your skills.

Trail Rides

We offer guided trail rides through the beautiful countryside of Roybon, France. Our experienced guides will take you on a journey through the forests, hills, and valleys of this beautiful region.

Horse Boarding

We offer horse boarding services for horse owners who need a safe and comfortable place to keep their horses. Our stables are clean, well-maintained, and staffed by experienced professionals.
