About Us

We are a family-owned horse riding company located in Roybon, France. We have been in the business for over 20 years and we take pride in providing the best horse riding experience to our customers. Our horses are well taken care of and our guides are experienced and knowledgeable.

We offer a variety of horse riding packages to suit your needs. Whether you want to go on a leisurely ride through the countryside or a more adventurous ride through the mountains, we have something for everyone.
